Vocabulary Overview

Before we start, let's review some new vocabulary that will be used in this lesson:

Sustantivos:

- Amigo/a - Friend

- Compañero/a - Companion, Partner

Verbos:

- Conocer - To meet, to know

- Presentar - To introduce

Expresiones:

- Mucho gusto - Nice to meet you

- Igualmente - Likewise

Conversation in Spanish

Ana: Hola, Juan. ¿Cómo estás?

Juan: Hola, Ana. Estoy bien, gracias. Te presento a mi amigo, Pedro.

Ana: ¡Hola, Pedro! Mucho gusto.

Pedro: Hola, Ana. Igualmente, mucho gusto.

Juan: Pedro y yo somos compañeros de trabajo.

Ana: ¡Qué bien! ¿Hace cuánto se conocen?

Pedro: Nos conocemos desde hace tres años.

Conversation in English

Ana: Hi, Juan. How are you?

Juan: Hi, Ana. I'm fine, thank you. Let me introduce you to my friend, Pedro.

Ana: Hi, Pedro! Nice to meet you.

Pedro: Hi, Ana. Likewise, nice to meet you.

Juan: Pedro and I are coworkers.

Ana: That's great! How long have you known each other?

Pedro: We've known each other for three years.

Activity 1:

In the conversation, Juan introduces Pedro to Ana. What did Juan say?

Part of the text: "Juan: Hola, Ana. Estoy bien, gracias. Te presento a mi amigo, Pedro."

Activity 2:

How long have Juan and Pedro known each other?

Part of the text: "Pedro: Nos conocemos desde hace tres años."

Activity 3:

What is the relationship between Juan and Pedro?

Part of the text: "Juan: Pedro y yo somos compañeros de trabajo."
